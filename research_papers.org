Convolutional Neural Nets

[[https://arxiv.org/pdf/1511.00561.pdf][link]] A Deep Convolutional Encoder-Decoder Architecture for Image Segmentation. Kendall et Al.
[[https://mi.eng.cam.ac.uk/projects/segnet/][examples]] of scene understanding

Gameplay Agents

[[https://tomaxent.com/2017/07/07/Demystifying-Deep-Reinforcement-Learning/][link]] Demystifying Deep Reinforcement Learning. Someone else's blog post.
Concise guide on Reinforcement Learning that highlights the following topics `Reinforcement Learning`, `Markov process`, `Rewards`, `Q-learning`, `Exploration-Exploitation`, and `Deep Q-Learning`.

[[https://www.nature.com/articles/nature14236.pdf][link]] Human level control through deep reinforcement learning. Mnih et Al. Deepmind.
More thorough follow up to [[https://arxiv.org/pdf/1312.5602v1.pdf][previous deepmind atari paper]]. One of the most cited Reinforcement Learning papers.

[[https://arxiv.org/pdf/1901.10995.pdf][link]] Go-explore. Ecoffet et Al.
Montezuma's Revenge and Pitfall at superhuman level. Exploits `starting over` from promising points in its exploration to learn a policy.
